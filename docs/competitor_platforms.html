Freelance Platforms for Software Developers

Research report for claude_upwork — Generated 2026-03-28

General Freelance Marketplaces

Freelancer.com

One of the largest general freelance marketplaces. Supports contests, fixed-price, and hourly projects.

Model:Bidding on posted jobs + contest system Freelancer Fee:10% or $5 (whichever is greater) Best For:Wide range of dev work; good for beginners building a portfolio Vetting:None
+ Lower fees than Upwork, contest model to showcase skills
- Lower average budgets, race-to-the-bottom pricing

Fiverr

Gig-based marketplace where freelancers create service listings that clients browse and purchase directly.

Model:Seller-driven gig listings (no bidding) Freelancer Fee:20% Best For:Productized dev services (landing pages, scripts, bug fixes) Vetting:None
+ No bidding — clients come to you; passive lead generation
- High fee (20%), perception as a "cheap" platform

PeoplePerHour

UK-based marketplace combining gig-style "Hourlies" with traditional project proposals.

Model:Hybrid — gig listings + bidding on projects Freelancer Fee:20% on first $700, 7.5% to $7K, 3.5% above Best For:Web/WordPress dev; strong UK/European client base Vetting:Light
+ Dual model flexibility, strong European market
- Smaller marketplace, tiered fees reset per project

Guru.com

Long-running marketplace with workroom-based collaboration and flexible payment options.

Model:Bidding + workroom project management Freelancer Fee:5–9% (based on membership tier) Best For:General software/web dev; budget-conscious freelancers Vetting:None
+ Lowest open-marketplace fees (5%), no Connects system
- Smaller client pool, dated interface

Developer-Specific Platforms

Toptal

Elite vetted network claiming top 3% of applicants. Focused on senior developers, designers, and finance experts.

Model:Matching/placement (no bidding) Freelancer Fee:0% — margin from client rate (typically 40-60%) Best For:Senior/expert devs commanding $60–200+/hr Vetting:Rigorous multi-step screening
+ Much higher rates, pre-vetted clients, no bidding wars
- Very difficult to get accepted, less project choice flexibility

Arc.dev (formerly CodementorX)

Vetted developer marketplace for freelance and full-time remote roles with growing companies.

Model:Matching — contract, contract-to-hire, full-time Freelancer Fee:0% — clients pay Arc Best For:Mid-senior devs; web, mobile, cloud Vetting:Technical assessment
+ No freelancer fees, supports full-time transition
- Must pass vetting, smaller opportunity volume

Gun.io

Curated freelance platform for senior developers, emphasizing long-term engagements.

Model:Matching (no bidding) Freelancer Fee:0% — clients pay Gun.io Best For:Senior engineers (5+ yr); full-stack, backend, DevOps Vetting:Yes
+ Zero fees, curated clients, longer engagements
- Selective, fewer opportunities, less visibility until matched

Turing.com

AI-powered platform matching vetted remote devs with US companies for long-term roles.

Model:AI-driven matching (no bidding) Freelancer Fee:0% — margin model; rates $30–80+/hr Best For:Mid-senior devs seeking stable long-term remote contracts Vetting:AI-driven skills assessment
+ Stable long-term work, AI-matched, remote-first
- Often full-time commitment required, less project variety

X-Team

Developer community and staffing platform with strong emphasis on culture and perks.

Model:Placement into client teams Freelancer Fee:0% + learning/conference budget included Best For:Mid-senior devs; JS/TS, React, Node, mobile, DevOps Vetting:Yes (selective)
+ Community perks, stable placements, developer-friendly
- Very selective, limited project choice

Lemon.io

Vetted marketplace connecting startups with senior freelance developers. 48-hour matching.

Model:Matching (accept/decline) Freelancer Fee:0% — clients pay Lemon.io; rates $25–80/hr Best For:3+ yr experience; React, Node, Python, Ruby, mobile Vetting:Technical + English + soft skills
+ No fees, startup-focused, fast matching
- Must pass vetting, smaller opportunity pool

Enterprise / High-End Consulting

Catalant (formerly HourlyNerd)

Enterprise consulting marketplace for strategic and technical projects with Fortune 500 clients.

Model:Project-based matching Freelancer Fee:~15–20% (client-side or margin) Best For:Senior consultants/architects; $150–400+/hr engagements Vetting:Yes
+ Fortune 500 clients, high-value projects
- Not for junior devs, more consulting than coding

A.Team

Invite-only network of elite product builders assembled into cross-functional "mission" teams.

Model:Team missions (no individual bidding) Freelancer Fee:0% — margin model; rates $150–300+/hr Best For:Elite senior devs; product engineering, AI/ML, growth Vetting:Invite-only
+ Premium rates, curated teams, high-impact projects
- Hard to get in, limited mission availability

Braintrust

Decentralized, user-owned talent network. Web3/token governance. Enterprise clients like Nike and NASA.

Model:Matching + apply (community-governed) Freelancer Fee:0% — clients pay 15% Best For:Mid-senior devs; engineering, data, DevOps Vetting:Yes
+ Zero freelancer fees, enterprise clients, transparent governance
- Web3 model unfamiliar to some, competitive for top roles

Niche / Emerging Platforms

Contra

Commission-free freelance platform with portfolio-style profiles. Positioning as the modern Upwork alternative.

Model:Portfolio/direct hire + job applications Freelancer Fee:0% Best For:All levels; web dev, mobile, design-adjacent engineering Vetting:None
+ Zero fees, modern UX, portfolio-centric
- Much smaller client base, still building marketplace liquidity

Wellfound (formerly AngelList Talent)

Startup-focused job and freelance platform. Connects developers with startups for contract and full-time roles.

Model:Direct hire — profiles with rate expectations Freelancer Fee:0% Best For:Startup ecosystem devs; contract-to-hire, equity roles Vetting:None
+ Free, startup access, potential for equity
- More full-time oriented, less payment protection

Working Not Working

Curated creative and technical talent network used by major brands and agencies.

Model:Availability toggle — clients browse and reach out Freelancer Fee:0% Best For:Creative technologists, front-end/interactive devs Vetting:Curated membership
+ High-profile clients, no fees, creative tech focus
- Niche, smaller volume, must be accepted

Flexiple

Vetted freelance developer network focused on placing top talent for part-time or full-time contracts.

Model:Matching (accept/decline) Freelancer Fee:0% Best For:3+ yr experience; React, Node, Python, Ruby, mobile Vetting:Multi-step (technical + soft skills)
+ No fees, flexible part-time options, personalized matching
- Must pass vetting, smaller volume, less well-known

Gigster

Managed project delivery platform assembling vetted teams for enterprise software builds.

Model:Managed teams — Gigster scopes + assembles Freelancer Fee:0% — paid per project/hourly by Gigster Best For:Senior devs; full-stack, AI/ML, blockchain, enterprise Vetting:Yes
+ No sales required, managed projects, enterprise work
- Less autonomy, Gigster controls pricing and client relationship
